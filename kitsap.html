Flow Line and Riparian Shade Modeling

in East Kitsap County

Working with the Suquamish Indian Tribe, I use Python scripting and ArcGIS Pro geoprocessing tools to accurately model potential stream lines in East Kitsap County. Following this modeling, I will use another Python script to depict shade estimations based on tree canopy cover.

The purpose of this modeling is to identify potential streams in the County, as well as provide guidance to prioritize riparian area restoration, management, and conservation.
