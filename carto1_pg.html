Nooksack River Watershed:

Major Rivers and Transborder Floodplains

This map is published by the Border Policy Research Institute in one of their policy brief reports. The focus is to highlight the transboundary nature of environmental issues, particularly flooding, in the Nooksack River region. This includes floodplains, rivers, and other ecosystems extending across the border between Canada and the United States. This map was created with fellow cartographer, Kailey Schillinger-Brokaw.
