Teaching

Introductory GIS

This course provides an introduction to Geographic Information Science (GIS) and ArcGIS software. Students gain a technical and conceptual understanding of GIS and how to work with geospatial data and visualizations. This 4-credit course with lab is the foundation for the WWU GIS Major, Minor, and Certificate.

Cartography

This course teaches cartographic and graphic design techniques and skills for effectively communicating with spatial data. Students learn foundational and advanced cartographic techniques, data visualization methods, and how to utilize online, interactive maps. This course also serves as an introduction to the limitations and ethics of graphic communication. This is a 5-credit course with lab.

Intermediate GIS

This technical course focuses on professional civil engineering and surveying applications of beginner to intermediate-level GIS using ArcGIS software. Students learn how to design analysis and interpret results from field-based mapping methods, like ESRI FieldMaps. This is a 5-credit course with lab.

Workshops

I teach workshops to provide GIS and cartography training. These workshops focus on the ESRI ArcGIS Suite, particularly Pro and Online, as well as GPS training.
